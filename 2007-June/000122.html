Hi Didou,

Enclosed you will find four beryl files which I have just translated into Polish plus emerald.po. It was really hard work to accomplish. Actually I was the second translator, not the first one for Polish localization. Nevertheless I corrected many fuzzy or improper translations and added a really huge amount of not translated positions. The final result is still not satisfactory due to strange problems with many words which do not want to appear in Polish in spite of translating them duly using KBabel. All concerned files have been downloaded from original Beryl archives and fully updated using intltools-update pl command at console. I suppose that remaining English words in translated po files it is common problem for all localizations. Therefore I am really surprised there is no information about it till now at our localization forum or mailing-list. A few days ago I have sent a mail to Beryl developers advising them about translation problems and have got a reply which does not explain actually nothing how to solve them. Anyway it is very interesting because maybe my (pardon, our) efforts are useless taking into account that Beryl would be only history soon and we will be forced to start again with translating a new 3D Desktop Manager named Compiz-Fusion.

Below you will find my mail and reply from Beryl i18n team head (Jigish 'CyberOrg' Gohil) as I would like to avoid repeating to write the same thing twice:

Would be nice to hear from you what do you think about it?

Best regards - Uznamer
